Operators and Expressions

Operators are symbols that perform operations on variables and values. Expressions are combinations of variables, operators, and values that yield a result.

Types of Operators

  • Arithmetic Operators: +, -, *, /, %
  • Assignment Operators: =, +=, -=, *=, /=
  • Comparison Operators: ==, !=, >, <, >=, <=
  • Logical Operators: &&, ||, !
  • Bitwise Operators: &, |, ^, ~, <<, >>
  • Unary Operators: ++, --
